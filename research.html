Trace Mining and Data Visualization

In time-critical and more importantly safety-critical embedded software, it is crucial to monitor the behaviour of the system and see whether it deviates from its specifications, i.e., behaves abnormally. One way to monitor this kind of behaviour is through observing partial information of the execution of the system, which is possible using its execution trace. In this research, we are trying to develop techniques to detect anomaly, i.e., abnormal behavior in generic embedded software.

Our paper SiPTA: Signal Processing for Trace-based Anomaly Detection has been accepted in EMSOFT 2014, International Conference on Embedded Systems ( List of Accepted Papers).

About Me


I am a Software Engineer currently pursuing my Master's of Applied Science with the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at the University of Waterloo. My research focuses on system tracing, statistical trace analysis, data visualisation for safety critical systems and evidence driven certification.

I am also the co-founder of Acerta. We aim to accelerate the safety certification process for automobiles, airplanes, medical devices, and many other safety critical systems. Acerta relies on system traces generated during system runtime and extracts evidence that the system is behaving correctly.

I am interested in building software for web applications, distributed systems, embedded and safety-critical systems, and robotics.
